Who We Are

Addis Med Link is a non-profit organization that was established in 2023 under the guidance of Blue Health Ethiopia. We are a group of students from more than 5 schools in Addis Ababa.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission is to make critical medication medication accessible for low-incom Ethiopians who are battling chronc illnesses.


Our Vision is to be the most impactful initiative that provides medication to low-income Ethiopians living with chronic illnesses. We envision a future where every individual has equal access to essential medication.

What We Do

Our aim is to create a lasting impact through a monthly giving program where medications are bought with the money donated each month from member donors, fostering a community-driven and sustainable solution. Empowering beneficiaries is a priority, achieved through educational initiatives emphasizing the importance of proper medication intake. Ensuring utmost transparency, we partner with important stakeholders in the field for accurate and responsible medication distribuiton.
